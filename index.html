TECHNICAL GRAPHICS GUIDE

Path Tracing & PBR
  • Showcase Path Tracing: The sphere showcase now uses a real WGSL compute path tracer for GI, reflections, and shadow bounce.
  • Emission Nodes: Specific model parts emit light (e.g., eyes, tech).
  • Normal Maps: Simulation of high-resolution surface depth on low-poly geometry.
  • Roughness/Metallic: Controls reflection sharpness and metallic response.
Hero Lighting & Gobos

Hero Lighting: Ambient fill light that follows the character to ensure visibility in dark interiors (Horizon Forbidden West style).
Light Gobos: Stencil textures on lights creating complex shadow patterns.

Performance & SM

The showcase path tracer is progressive and resolves over time while the camera is still. Leon gameplay stays on the faster hybrid WebGPU stack so movement remains responsive.

HDR and Brightness

The atmospheric art style is intentionally dark. If the image appears washed out or overly dark, please use the three-tier brightness system for manual calibration.

Lighting Artifacts

Obstructive white light may occur during puzzle interactions if Field of View (FOV) is modified outside of standard limits.
